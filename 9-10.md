## 📍 Location 09 — "The Airport Goodbye"

![Location 09](scenes/airport/bg.svg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Tier** | Late Game Emotional Boss Scene |
| **Tone** | Heavy, cinematic, unresolved |
| **Vibe** | Love vs timing |

### ✈️ Scene Concept

Airport terminal at night.

- Departure boards glowing
- Rolling luggage sounds
- People hugging goodbye everywhere
- One gate glowing in the background

**This is a time-pressure emotional arena.** The world is moving. They aren't ready.

### 🎯 Core Player Goal

**Decide whether love fights time… or not.**

This is about: sacrifice · regret · bravery · avoidance. **No neutral outcomes.**

### 🧠 Mechanics Introduced

- Countdown mechanic
- Last words system
- Irreversible choices
- Long-term regret flags

### 🎮 Interaction Hotspots

**🛫 Departure Board** — Shows "BOARDING". Player can: stall · push urgency · ignore reality. Triggers tension scaling.

**🎒 The Bag Grab** — One of the most iconic choices in the game.

- Take the bag (stay)
- Hand it back (leave)
- Hold it together (indecision path)

**📢 Final Boarding Call** — Audio-style trigger moment. Player must act: confess · joke · freeze. *Major branching node.*

**🚶 The Walk Away** *(Secret)* — Let one of them walk without stopping them → unlocks bittersweet solo arcs later

### 🧨 Secrets

- **"Run Back Scene"** — If player lets them go but builds enough past tension… they turn around. Late-game redemption unlock
- **"Unsaid Words"** — If player stays silent at key moments, future scenes contain ghost dialogue. Extremely powerful storytelling mechanic

### 💔 Possible Outcomes

- Stay together arc
- Long-distance love arc
- Bittersweet separation
- Regret timeline
- "Right person, wrong time" flag

### 🔐 Future DLC Hooks

- Reunion years later
- Alternate timeline endings
- Parallel life arcs
- Memory flashback mechanics

## 📍 Location 10 — "The Hospital Waiting Room"

![Location 10](scenes/hospital/bg.svg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Tier** | Late Game Soul Scene |
| **Tone** | Quiet, raw, human |
| **Vibe** | Nothing to hide anymore |

### 🏥 Scene Concept

A dim hospital waiting room at night.

- Harsh overhead lighting
- Vending machine hum
- Plastic chairs
- Coffee gone cold
- Silence that feels loud

**This is where all masks fall off.**

### 🎯 Core Player Goal

**Reveal who they really are to each other.**

This location strips away: ego · flirtation · games. Only truth survives here.

### 🧠 Mechanics Introduced

- Care vs fear system
- Emotional authenticity meter
- Silent bonding mechanics
- Protective instinct triggers

### 🎮 Interaction Hotspots

**☕ Cold Coffee Cup**

- Bring them a new one (care)
- Sit beside silently (presence)
- Leave the room (avoidance)  
- **Defines:** Emotional maturity stats

**🪑 The Empty Chair Gap** — Physical distance matters here.

- Close the gap
- Stay apart
- Let them come to you  
- *Subtle but huge consequences*

**📱 Phone Call Moment** — One gets a call with news. Player decides: answer for them · let it ring · take the phone away. *Massive trust mechanic.*

**🚪 ICU Door** *(Secret)* — Clickable but unreachable. If triggered → unlocks deep internal monologue scenes

### 🧨 Secrets

- **"Hands on Knees"** — If both are sitting and player waits… they slowly lean toward each other. No dialogue. Just presence. One of the most powerful quiet bonding triggers
- **"Truth Without Words"** — If player avoids all dramatic choices → unlocks pure connection route. Extremely rare path

### 💞 Possible Outcomes

- Deep unbreakable bond
- Emotional awakening
- Fear-driven distance
- Role reversal arcs (one becomes caregiver later)
- Permanent life-changing flags

### 🔐 Future DLC Hooks

- Recovery arc
- Loss arc
- Parenthood arcs
- Time skip chapters
- "We survived something together" bond
